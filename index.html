Passions

My passion for photography has always been an integral part of who I am. From a young age, I found myself captivated by the artistry and storytelling potential behind a single photograph. It was as if each image had the power to transport me to another time, another place, and evoke a myriad of emotions within me. One particular aspect of photography that has captivated my heart is the art of portrait photography. There is something incredibly intimate and profound about capturing the essence of a person through a single image. The human face, with all its intricacies and nuances, is a canvas waiting to be explored and celebrated.

During my time in university, my passion for photography grew even deeper. I immersed myself in the study of various techniques, learning to master the technical aspects while simultaneously exploring the boundless creativity that the medium offered. From black and white film photography to digital manipulation, I eagerly experimented with different styles and approaches, allowing myself to be guided by the invisible thread of my artistic vision.
